Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before attending DevConf 2026.

What is DevConf 2026?

DevConf 2026 is a three-day developer conference featuring world-class speakers, hands-on workshops, networking sessions, and the latest technologies.

Can I buy tickets at the venue?

Yes, but tickets are limited. We recommend purchasing online to guarantee your seat.

Will all sessions be recorded?

Yes. All attendees receive access to session recordings after the conference.

Is food included with my ticket?

Yes. Lunch, snacks, and coffee breaks are included each day.

Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?

Yes. Tickets can be transferred before the registration deadline.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes. Students receive a special discounted rate with a valid student ID.

Will there be networking sessions?

Absolutely! Meet developers, founders, recruiters, and industry leaders throughout the conference.

How can I become a speaker?

Submit your proposal through our Call for Speakers page before the deadline.
